# Cold Email — Quality Criteria

Score a short cold / outreach email. Anchors: **50 = average, 70 = good, 90+ =
exceptional.** Reward craft, not length — the best email is often shorter. Award the
top of a band when the email clearly does the right thing.

## Dimensions (total: 100)

### Hook & relevance (30)
- Opens with something specific to THIS recipient — not a generic "I wanted to reach
  out" or "I think our product could help" (0-18)
- The first line earns the second; no throat-clearing (0-12)

### Concrete value (25)
- States a specific, believable outcome — not "does a lot of things" or "a great
  fit" (0-15)
- One claim made concrete: a number, a named pain, a before/after (0-10)

### Brevity & clarity (20)
- Tight — every sentence earns its place; no filler or hedging (0-12)
- Reads in one breath; plain-spoken, not corporate (0-8)

### Call to action (15)
- One clear, low-friction ask (0-10)
- Specific, not "let me know what works for you" (0-5)

### Voice (10)
- Human and confident; never salesy or obsequious ("Thanks so much!") (0-10)
