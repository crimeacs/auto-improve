# Voice-Note Quality Criteria

A 30-second, first-person voice note in which a founder explains the gist
of their own research paper to a sharp friend. It will be READ ALOUD by TTS, so it
must sound spoken, not written.

Score for genuine craft, not absence-of-flaws. Anchors: ~50 average (clear but
generic/written-sounding), ~70 good/ship-able (spoken, real specifics, clean,
lands), 90+ exceptional (vivid hook, earned image, every word pulls, punch
ending). Award the full points of a band when the note clearly does the right
thing — the way from 70 to 90 is SHARPER CRAFT (better hook, more physical image,
tighter landing), NOT merely removing mistakes.

## Dimensions (total: 100)

### Hook (20 points)
- Grabs attention in the first ~3 seconds / first sentence. AWARD THE FULL 12 for a vivid, concrete opener that makes a sharp friend lean in (0-12)
- No throat-clearing ("In this paper", "Today I want to talk about", "So basically"). AWARD THE FULL 8 when the note opens straight into the idea (0-8)

### Spoken cadence (20 points)
- Sounds natural read aloud: short sentences, contractions, one breath each (0-10)
- Not list-like or written-prose; no semicolons, no bullet feel (0-10)

### Concreteness (20 points)
- Uses the paper's real specifics — a method/architecture name, a benchmark/metric,
  a dataset and its scale (e.g. DPRNN, SI-SDR, STEAD ~400k for a seismology paper;
  the equivalent named specifics for any other topic) (0-12)
- Vivid and physical over abstract — an earned analogy or image ("two earthquakes arriving on top of each other") (0-8)

### No AI-tells (15 points)
- No "delve", "leverage", "in conclusion", "it's worth noting", "this approach" filler (0-8)
- No hedging or robotic transitions ("Furthermore", "Moreover", "Additionally") (0-7)

### Brevity (15 points)
- ~70-95 words (~30s spoken). AWARD THE FULL 10 when within this band; penalize >110 hard, <50 thin (0-10)
- AWARD THE FULL 5 when nothing could be cut without loss; every sentence earns its place (0-5)

### Landing (10 points)
- AWARD THE FULL 10 for an ending that reframes/raises the stakes — a memorable
  concrete punch or image; ~6-7 clean-but-ordinary; ~3 fade-out (0-10)
